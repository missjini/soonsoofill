PROCESS

THE 9 PROCESS

The hyaluronic acid filler e.p.t.q. produced according to
“The 9 Process” promises an exquisite product that meets
safety standards.

  • Safe Raw Material

    e.p.t.q.‘s raw material is produced by
    exclusive manufacturing line,
    which is strictly managed.

  • HA Concentration

    SOON SOO FILL has 24mg of
    hyaluronic acid.

  • High Viscoelasticity

    SOON SOO FILL is maintained in skin
    tissue with high viscoelasticity.

  • Technology of Particle
    Homogenization

    Homogeneous particle is implemented
    through Downing Process.

  • No Detection of Residual BDDE

    Residual BDDE is not detected.

  • Aseptic Environment

    SOON SOO FILL manufactured under
    sterile and aseptic condition.

  • Less than 0.1 EU/ml of
    Endotoxin

    SOON SOO FILL can control endotoxin
    level below 0.1 EU/ml.

  • Similar pH and Osmotic
    Pressure

    SOON SOO FILL delivers similar pH and
    osmotic pressure as the human body.

  • Extrusion Force

    Extrusion force is managed by each batch.
