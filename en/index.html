TECHNOLOGY

Jetema continues to study for the safety improvement with the maximum goal.
Pure HA gel : pure raw material, washing method / 4P
Safe bridging technique : maximizing bridging efficiency, minimizing BDDE input (low MoD – checking stability party) and high viscoelasticity cohesion (6P, 7P)
Clinical outcomes : researchers’ evaluation and patients’ satisfaction (8P, 9P)
